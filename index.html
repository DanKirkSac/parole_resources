    • Welcome Home!

    Getting out of prison or jail can be both the greatest experience of our lives, and the scariest. Some of us parole with all of the resources we'll ever need, and some with nothing. Whether you have all the resources you need, or need all the resources you can get, you'll find something to help here.

    Employment & Education Services

    Finding employment friendly to felons is not easy, but luckily there's a lot of help out there. From companies that provide daily pay for picking up trash on the freeways (and they help with finding full time jobs too!) to companies providing full time employment you'll find them all listed here.

    Housing Services

    There are a lot of housing programs available to people on parole or probation. Most are offered through a wide variety of programs. Each program has its own requirements. What happens after a transition program is finished, though? There are still plenty of options for low-cost housing available in Sacramento.
